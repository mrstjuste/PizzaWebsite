Our little Story !

Welcome to Empire Pizza is a small Italian pizza shop that is located in the heart of Little Italy. It was founded by a family of Italian immigrants who were passionate about sharing their love for authentic Italian cuisine with their community.

Empire Pizza specializes in large, hand-crafted pizzas made with only the freshest ingredients. The shop prides itself on using only the highest quality mozzarella cheese and ripe, juicy tomatoes for its sauces.

The mission of Empire Pizza is to provide customers with a taste of Italy right here in the United States. We strive to create pizzas that are authentic, delicious, and made with care and attention to detail. Our goal is to become a go-to spot for those who love Italian food and are looking for a satisfying meal that will leave them full and satisfied.

We hope you enjoy our products!
